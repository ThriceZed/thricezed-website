    1 · Stand still

    Pick your spot and stay on it. Hold the phone close to your body and pivot in place. The whole sphere gets photographed from one point.

    2 · Aim at the circles

    Floating circles surround you. Line the reticle up with a circle and hold steady. The shot fires by itself when you are aligned. The glowing circle is the recommended next one.

    3 · Cover the sphere

    Work around the horizon first, then the upper and lower rings, then straight up and straight down. The map in the corner shows what you have covered.

    4 · Process and export

    When every circle is filled, TZHDRI merges exposures, stitches the panorama, and exports HDR, EXR, TIFF, and JPEG for your 3D package.
